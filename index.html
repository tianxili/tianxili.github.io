I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at the University of Virginia. My research is mainly about

  • statistical network analysis

  • statistical learning

Before coming to UVa, I obtained my PhD in statistics from the University of Michigan, advised by Prof. Liza Levina and Prof. Ji Zhu.
Previously, I also worked as an applied researcher in Microsoft (2012-2013), focusing on online controlled experiments (A/B testing).
